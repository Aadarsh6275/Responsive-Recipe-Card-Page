Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • Black pepper

Preparation:

Cook spaghetti until al dente. In a bowl, whisk eggs and cheese. Mix cooked pasta with pancetta then quickly combine with egg mixture off the heat. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.

Tacos

Delicious Mexican dish made with folded or rolled tortillas filled with various toppings.

Ingredients:

  • 8 small tortillas
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • Cheese, lettuce, and salsa for topping

Preparation:

Cook ground beef with onion until browned. Stir in taco seasoning. Warm tortillas and fill with beef mixture. Top with cheese, lettuce, and salsa of choice.

Avocado Toast

Healthy and quick breakfast made with toasted bread topped with mashed avocado and fresh toppings.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation:

Toast the bread slices. Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Spread avocado on toast. Top with sliced cherry tomatoes and herbs as desired.
